About

My name is Vladimir Sajanek and I was born in Slovakia. I currently live and work in Stockholm, Sweden.

Most of my artworks have abstract, modern or geometrical feel to it as its inspired by urban architecture, different landscapes, people and animals. In my paintings and sketches I try to capture the feelings of different compositions. I do not use only one painting style because I love switching between different styles all the time so I am able to create something new, something heartwarming, something different.

The diversity of influences in my life include impressionism, perspective, cubism, modern art, abstract art & pop art. With beautiful colors and texture I want to give each painting a unique mood and depth.

I also express the energy of different compositions by using different painting techniques, by applying paint with a palette knife and gestural brush-strokes and by using a mix of geometrical shapes. Colors are also very important to me because I want to achieve a harmony by combining warm and cold colors. I use a wide variety of tools and materials to create art pieces in a variety of sizes. I primarily work with acrylics, watercolours, enamel, inks as well as advanced digital technology. Some of my art works are in a private collections.
